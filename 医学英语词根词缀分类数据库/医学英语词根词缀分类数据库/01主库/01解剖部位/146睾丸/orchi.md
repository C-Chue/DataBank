- <span class="definition">含义：睾丸</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：orchid, orchido, orchio, orchium</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词orkhis（意为testicle，即睾丸）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成睾丸相关词汇</span>
