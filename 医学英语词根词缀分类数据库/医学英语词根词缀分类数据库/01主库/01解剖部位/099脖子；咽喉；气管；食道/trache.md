- <span class="definition">含义：气管</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：tracheo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词trachea（意为rough或uneven，即粗糙或不平，后衍生为粗糙不平的体内管道）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成气管相关词汇</span>


---
