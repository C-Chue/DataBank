- <span class="definition">含义：纤维；结缔组织</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词inos（意为fiber，即纤维）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成纤维；结缔组织相关词汇</span>


---
