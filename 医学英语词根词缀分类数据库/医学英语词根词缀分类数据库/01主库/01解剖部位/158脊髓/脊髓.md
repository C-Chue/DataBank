
**<center>spin-3</center>**

- <span class="definition">含义：脊髓</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：spino</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词spina（意为thorn或spine，即刺或荆棘）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成脊髓相关词汇</span>
