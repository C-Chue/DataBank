- <span class="definition">含义：肌肉</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：myo, myos</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词mys（意为muscle，即肌肉）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成肌肉相关词汇</span>
