- <span class="definition">含义：肌肉</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词kreas（意为flesh，即肉）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成肌肉相关词汇</span>
- <span class="definition">说明：creatin比kreatin常用</span>
