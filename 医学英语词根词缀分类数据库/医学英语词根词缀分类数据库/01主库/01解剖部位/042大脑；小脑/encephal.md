- <span class="definition">含义：大脑</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：encephalo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词enkephalos（意为inside the head，即头的内部，例如大脑）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成大脑相关词汇</span>
