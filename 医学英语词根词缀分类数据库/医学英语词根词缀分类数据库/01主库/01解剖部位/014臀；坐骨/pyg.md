- <span class="definition">含义：臀；臀部</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：pygo, pyga, pygia</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词pugē（意为buttocks，即臀部）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成臀；臀部相关词汇</span>
