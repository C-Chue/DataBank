- <span class="definition">含义：眼睑</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：blepharo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词blepharon（意为eyelid，即眼皮或眼睑）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成眼睑相关词汇</span>
