- <span class="definition">含义：骨头</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：oss, osse, osteo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词os（意为bone，即骨头）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成骨头相关词汇</span>


---
