- <span class="definition">含义：颅骨</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：phreni, phreno, phrenia</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词phrēn（意为mind，即大脑或精神）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成颅骨相关词汇，不过phren此时不常用</span>
