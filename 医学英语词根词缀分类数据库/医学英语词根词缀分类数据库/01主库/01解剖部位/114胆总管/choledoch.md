- <span class="definition">含义：胆总管</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：choledocho</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词chole（意为bile，即胆汁）和doche（意为receiving，即接收）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成胆总管相关词汇</span>
