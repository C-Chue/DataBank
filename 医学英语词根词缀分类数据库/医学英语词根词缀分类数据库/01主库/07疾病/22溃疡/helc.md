- <span class="definition">含义：溃疡</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：helco, elc</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词helkos（意为ulcer或sore，即溃疡或疼痛）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成溃疡相关词汇</span>
