- <span class="definition">含义：肿瘤；癌症</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：oncus</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词onkos（意为mass或tumor，即肿块）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成肿瘤；癌症相关词汇</span>
