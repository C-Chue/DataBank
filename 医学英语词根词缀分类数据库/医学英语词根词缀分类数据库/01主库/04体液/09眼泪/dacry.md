- <span class="definition">含义：眼泪</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：dacryo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词dakry（意为tear或teardrop，即眼泪或泪珠）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成眼泪相关词汇</span>


---
