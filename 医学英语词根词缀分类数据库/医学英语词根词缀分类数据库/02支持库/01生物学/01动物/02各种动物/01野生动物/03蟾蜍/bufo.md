- <span class="definition">含义：蟾蜍</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词bufo（意为toad，即蟾蜍）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成蟾蜍相关词汇</span>
