- <span class="definition">含义：藻类</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：phyco</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词phykos（意为seaweed或algae，即海藻或藻类）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成藻类相关词汇</span>
