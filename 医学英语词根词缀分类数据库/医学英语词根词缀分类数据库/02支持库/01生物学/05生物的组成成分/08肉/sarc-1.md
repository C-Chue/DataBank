- <span class="definition">含义：肉</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：sarco</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词sarx（意为flesh或muscle，即肉或肌肉）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成肉相关词汇</span>
