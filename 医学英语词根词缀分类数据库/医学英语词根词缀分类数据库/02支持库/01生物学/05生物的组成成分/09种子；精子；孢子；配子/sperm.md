- <span class="definition">含义：精子</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：spermo, spermat, spermato</span>

<span class="vocabulary">**polyspermy**</span> ['pɒlɪˌspɜːmi] n. [胚胎] 多精入卵；多精受精
