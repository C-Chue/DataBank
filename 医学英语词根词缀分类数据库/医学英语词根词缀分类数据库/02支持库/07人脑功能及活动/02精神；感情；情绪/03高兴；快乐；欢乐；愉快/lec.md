- <span class="definition">含义：愉快</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：let, lic, lig</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词delicere（意为to entice或to charm，即引诱或吸引）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成愉快相关词汇</span>
