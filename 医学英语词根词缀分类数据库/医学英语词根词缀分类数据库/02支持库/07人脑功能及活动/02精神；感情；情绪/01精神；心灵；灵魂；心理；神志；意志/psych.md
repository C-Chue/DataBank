- <span class="definition">含义：心理；精神</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词psyche（意为breath、soul或mind，即呼吸、灵魂或精神）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成心理；精神相关词汇</span>

---
