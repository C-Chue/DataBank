<span class="vocabulary">**byssine**</span> [ˈbɪsɪn] adj. 细丝状的，棉絮状的

<span class="vocabulary">**byssinosis**</span> [ˌbɪsɪˈnəʊsɪs] n. 绵纤维吸入性肺炎
