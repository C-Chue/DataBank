- <span class="definition">含义：绳索；弦</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：chordo, cordo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词khordē（意为string或cord，即弦或绳索）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成绳索；弦相关词汇</span>
