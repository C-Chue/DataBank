- <span class="definition">含义：黑色</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：nigri, negro</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词niger（意为black或dark，即黑色）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成黑色相关词汇</span>
