- <span class="definition">含义：粘稠</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：visco, viscosi</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词viscum（意为birdlime，即粘鸟胶）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成粘稠相关词汇</span>

---
