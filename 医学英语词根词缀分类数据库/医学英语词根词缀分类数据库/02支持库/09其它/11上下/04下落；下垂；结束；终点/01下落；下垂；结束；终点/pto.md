- <span class="definition">含义：下落；下垂</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词ptosis（意为a falling或a falling down，即倒下）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成下落；下垂相关词汇</span>
