- <span class="definition">含义：尖端</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：apico</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词apex（意为tip或summit，即尖端或顶峰）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成尖端相关词汇</span>
