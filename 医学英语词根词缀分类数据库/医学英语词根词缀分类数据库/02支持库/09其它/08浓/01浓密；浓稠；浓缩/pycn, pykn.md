- <span class="definition">含义：浓密；浓稠</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：pycno, pykno</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词puknos（意为dense或thick，即浓密或浓稠）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成浓密；浓稠相关词汇</span>
