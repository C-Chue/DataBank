- <span class="definition">含义：溶解</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词solvere（意为to loosen或to dissolve，即松开或溶解）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成溶解相关词汇</span>
