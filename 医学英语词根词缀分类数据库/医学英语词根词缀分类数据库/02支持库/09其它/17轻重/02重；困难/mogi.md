- <span class="definition">含义：困难；费力</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：mogia</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词mogis（意为with difficulty或with effort，即困难或费力）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成困难；费力相关词汇</span>
