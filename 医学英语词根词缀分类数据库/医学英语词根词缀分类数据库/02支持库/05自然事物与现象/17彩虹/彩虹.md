
**<center>iri, irid, iris-1</center>**

- <span class="definition">含义：彩虹</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：irido</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词iris（意为rainbow，即彩虹）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成彩虹相关词汇</span>
