- <span class="definition">含义：发热</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：pyrexia, pyrexias</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词pyretos（意为fever，即发热）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成发热相关词汇</span>
