- <span class="definition">含义：活的；有生命力的</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词vivus（意为alive或living，即有生命力的或活的）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成活的；有生命力的相关词汇</span>

---
