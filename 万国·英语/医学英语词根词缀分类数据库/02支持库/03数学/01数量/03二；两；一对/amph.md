- <span class="definition">含义：两者</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：ampho, amphi</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词amphos（意为both或around，即两者或周边）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成两者相关词汇</span>
