
**<center>vern, vernal</center>**

- <span class="definition">含义：春天</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词ver（意为spring，即春天）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成春天相关词汇</span>
