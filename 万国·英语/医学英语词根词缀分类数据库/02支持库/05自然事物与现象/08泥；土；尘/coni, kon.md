- <span class="definition">含义：灰尘</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：conio, coniosis, conite, koni, konio, kono</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词konis（意为dust，即灰尘）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成灰尘相关词汇</span>
