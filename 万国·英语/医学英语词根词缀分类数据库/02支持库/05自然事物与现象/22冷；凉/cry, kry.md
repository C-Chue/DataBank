- <span class="definition">含义：冰冻；极冷</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：cryo, kryo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词kryos（意为cold或frost，即冷或霜）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成冰冻；极冷相关词汇</span>
