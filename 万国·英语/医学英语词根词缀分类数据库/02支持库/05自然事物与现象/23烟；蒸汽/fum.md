- <span class="definition">含义：烟；烟雾</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：fumi</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词fumus（意为smoke或fumes，即烟或烟雾）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成烟；烟雾相关词汇</span>
