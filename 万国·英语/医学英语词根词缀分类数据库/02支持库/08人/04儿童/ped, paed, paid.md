- <span class="definition">含义：儿童</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：pedo, paedo, paido</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词pais（意为child，即儿童）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成儿童相关词汇</span>
