- <span class="definition">含义：铃声；嗡嗡声</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词tinnitus（意为ringing或buzzing，即铃声或嗡嗡声）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成铃声；嗡嗡声相关词汇</span>
