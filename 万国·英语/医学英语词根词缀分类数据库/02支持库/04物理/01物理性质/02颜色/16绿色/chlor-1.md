- <span class="definition">含义：绿黄色；淡绿色</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：chloro</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词khlōros（意为greenish-yellow或pale green，即绿黄色或淡绿色）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成绿黄色；淡绿色相关词汇</span>
