- <span class="definition">含义：白色</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：leuco, leuko</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词leukos（意为white或bright，即白色或明亮）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成白色相关词汇</span>
