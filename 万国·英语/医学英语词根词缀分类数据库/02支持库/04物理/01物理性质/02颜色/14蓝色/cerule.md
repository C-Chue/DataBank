- <span class="definition">含义：蓝色；蔚蓝色</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：cerul</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词caeruleus（意为blue或azure，即蓝色或蔚蓝色）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成蓝色；蔚蓝色相关词汇</span>
