- <span class="definition">含义：黄色</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：lute, luteo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词lutum（意为mud或clay，即泥或黏土）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成黄色相关词汇</span>
