- <span class="definition">含义：杆状；棒状</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：rhabdo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词rhabdos（意为rod或stick，即棒子或棍子）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成杆状；棒状相关词汇</span>
