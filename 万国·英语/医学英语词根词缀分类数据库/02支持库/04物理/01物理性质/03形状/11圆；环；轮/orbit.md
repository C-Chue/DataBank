- <span class="definition">含义：圆形轨道；眼眶</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词orbis（意为circle或disk，即圆形或圆盘）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成圆形轨道；眼眶相关词汇</span>
