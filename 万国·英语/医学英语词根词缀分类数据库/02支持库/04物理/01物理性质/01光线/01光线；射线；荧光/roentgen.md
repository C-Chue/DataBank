- <span class="definition">含义：X射线</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：roentgeno</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：德国物理学家Wilhelm Conrad Roentgen（其在1895年发现了X射线）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成X射线相关词汇</span>
