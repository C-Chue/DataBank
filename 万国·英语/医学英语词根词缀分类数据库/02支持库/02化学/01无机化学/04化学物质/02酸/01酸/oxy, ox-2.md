- <span class="definition">含义：酸</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：oxia, oxic, oxal</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词oxys（意为sharp或acid，即酸的）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成酸相关词汇</span>
