
**<center>sulfa</center>**

- <span class="definition">含义：磺胺</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词sulphur（意为硫磺）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成磺胺相关词汇</span>
