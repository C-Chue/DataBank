
**<center>ampel</center>**

- <span class="definition">含义：葡萄藤</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：ampelo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词ampelos（意为vine或grapevine，即葡萄藤）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成葡萄藤相关词汇</span>
