- <span class="definition">含义：狭窄</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：sten</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词stenos（意为narrow或tight，即狭或紧）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成狭窄相关词汇</span>
