- <span class="definition">含义：速度；快</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：tacho, tach</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词tachys（意为swift或quick，即快或迅速）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成速度；快相关词汇</span>
