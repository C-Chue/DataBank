- <span class="definition">含义：干燥；干旱</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词xeros（意为dry，即干的）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成干燥；干旱相关词汇</span>

---
