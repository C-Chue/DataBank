- <span class="definition">含义：膨胀；扩张</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：ecta, ectat</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词ektasis（意为dilation或expansion，即膨胀或扩张）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成膨胀；扩张相关词汇</span>
