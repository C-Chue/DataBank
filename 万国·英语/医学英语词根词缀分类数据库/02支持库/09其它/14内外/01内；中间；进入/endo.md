- <span class="definition">含义：内部；里面</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：end</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词endon（意为within或inside，即内部或里面）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成内部；里面相关词汇</span>
