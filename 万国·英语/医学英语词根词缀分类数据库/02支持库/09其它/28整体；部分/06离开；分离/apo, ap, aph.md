- <span class="definition">含义：离开；分离</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊前缀apo（意为away from或separate，即离开或分离）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成离开；分离相关词汇</span>
