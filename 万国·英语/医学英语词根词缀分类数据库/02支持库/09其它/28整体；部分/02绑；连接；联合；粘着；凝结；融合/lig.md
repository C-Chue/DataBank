- <span class="definition">含义：捆绑；束缚</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：li, ly</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词ligare（意为to bind或to tie，即捆绑或束缚）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成捆绑；束缚相关词汇</span>
