- <span class="definition">含义：低矮；贴地；扁</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：chamae</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词chamai（意为on the ground或low-growing，即地面上或低矮的）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成低矮或贴地的植物或动物；扁相关词汇</span>
