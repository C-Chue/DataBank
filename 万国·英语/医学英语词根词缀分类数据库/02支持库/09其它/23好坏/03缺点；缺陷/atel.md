- <span class="definition">含义：不完美；不完整；不全</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：atelo, ateli</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词atēlos（意为imperfect或incomplete，即不完美或不完整）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成不完美；不完整；不全相关词汇</span>
