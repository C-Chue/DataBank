- <span class="definition">含义：缝合术</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：rhapho, rhaphy, rrhaphy</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词rhaptein（意为to sew或to stitch，即缝纫或缝合）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成缝合术相关词汇</span>
