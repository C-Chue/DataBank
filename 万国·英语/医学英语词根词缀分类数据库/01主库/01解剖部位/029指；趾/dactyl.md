- <span class="definition">含义：手指；脚趾</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：dactylo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词daktylos（意为finger或toe，即手指或脚趾）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成手指；脚趾相关词汇</span>
