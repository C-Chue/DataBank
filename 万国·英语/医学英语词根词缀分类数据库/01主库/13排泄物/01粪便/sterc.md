- <span class="definition">含义：粪便</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：sterco, stercor</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词stercus（意为dung或excrement，即粪便）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成粪便相关词汇</span>
