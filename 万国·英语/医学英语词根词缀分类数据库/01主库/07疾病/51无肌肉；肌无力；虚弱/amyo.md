- <span class="definition">含义：无肌肉；肌无力</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊前缀a（意为not或without，即否定）和希腊单词mys（意为muscle，即肌肉）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成无肌肉；肌无力相关词汇</span>
