- <span class="definition">含义：（皮肤或黏膜的）溃疡</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：ulcer</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词ulcus（意为sore或wound，即疼痛或伤口）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成（皮肤或黏膜的）溃疡相关词汇</span>
