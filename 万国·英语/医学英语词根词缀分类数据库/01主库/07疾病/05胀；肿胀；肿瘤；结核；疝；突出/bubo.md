- <span class="definition">变体：bubon</span>

<span class="vocabulary">**bubonic**</span> [bjuː'bɒnɪk] adj. 腹股沟腺炎的   

<span class="vocabulary">**bubonic plague**</span> [bjuːˌbɒnɪk ˈpleɪɡ] 腺鼠疫，腹股沟淋巴结鼠疫
