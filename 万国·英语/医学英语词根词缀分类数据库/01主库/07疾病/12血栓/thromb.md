- <span class="definition">含义：血栓</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：thrombo</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词thrombos（意为clot或clump，即凝块或聚集）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成血栓相关词汇</span>
