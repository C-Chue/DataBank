- <span class="definition">含义：疼痛</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：odyno, odynia, odynic,odyne, dyne, dynia</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词odyne（意为pain，即疼痛）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成疼痛相关词汇</span>
