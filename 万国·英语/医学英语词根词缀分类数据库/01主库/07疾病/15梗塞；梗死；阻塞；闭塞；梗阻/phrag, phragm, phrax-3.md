
- <span class="definition">含义：阻塞；闭塞；梗阻</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：phragmo, phraxis</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词phragma（意为fence或partition，即栅栏或分隔）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成阻塞；闭塞；梗阻相关词汇</span>
