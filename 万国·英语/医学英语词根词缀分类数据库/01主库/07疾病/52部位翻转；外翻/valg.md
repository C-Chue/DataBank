- <span class="definition">含义：肢体外翻</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词valgus（意为bow-legged或knock-kneed，即弓形腿、罗圈腿或膝外翻）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成肢体外翻相关词汇</span>
