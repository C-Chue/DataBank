- <span class="definition">含义：小脓疱</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：无</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：拉丁单词pustula（意为a small blister or pimple filled with pus，即小脓疱）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成小脓疱相关词汇</span>
