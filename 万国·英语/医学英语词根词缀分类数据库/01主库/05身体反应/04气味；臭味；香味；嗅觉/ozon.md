- <span class="definition">含义：臭味</span>
- <span class="definition">变体：ozono, ozoni, ozo, oz</span>
- <span class="definition">来源：希腊单词ozon（意为smell，即气味）</span>
- <span class="definition">用途：构成臭味相关词汇（常构成臭氧相关单词）</span>
